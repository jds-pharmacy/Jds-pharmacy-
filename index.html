Glycomet 500 SR

Medicine Details

Brand: USV Ltd

Batch No: SIF2784

Expiry Date: 02/2027

Composition: Metformin Hydrochloride SR 500 mg

Uses

Used to control blood sugar in type 2 diabetes. Sometimes also prescribed for PCOS.

How It Works

Improves insulin sensitivity and reduces glucose production in the liver.

Dosage

Take as prescribed by your doctor, usually once or twice daily with food.

Side Effects

Nausea, diarrhea, metallic taste. Rarely lactic acidosis (serious).

Important Notes

Do not use if you have severe kidney/liver disease or before imaging tests with contrast.
